## Creating an Account

### Registration Steps

1. **Click "Register" or "Sign Up"**
   - Look for the registration button in the top-right corner of the page

2. **Fill in Your Information**
   - **First Name:** Your given name
   - **Last Name:** Your family name
   - **Email Address:** A valid email (used for login and notifications)

   - **Password:** At least 6 characters (mix letters and numbers for security)

3. **Submit Registration**
   - Click the "Register" or "Create Account" button
   - Wait for confirmation message

4. **Success!**
   - You'll be automatically logged in
   - Your account is now active

### Registration Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Use a valid email address you check regularly
- Choose a strong password
- Remember your username

❌ **Don't:**
- Use someone else's email
- Share your password
- Use the same password you use elsewhere

## Browsing Properties

### Method 1: Browse by Neighborhood

The homepage displays Athens' most popular neighborhoods:

**Available Areas:**

**To Browse:**
1. Click on any neighborhood card
2. View all available properties in that area
3. Each property shows:
   - Property image
   - Price (€)
   - Number of bedrooms
   - Number of bathrooms
   - Size (square meters)

### Method 2: Filter by Listing Type *(if available)*

**For Sale vs. For Rent**
- Toggle between "For Sale" and "For Rent" at the top of the listings page
- Results update to show only properties of the selected type

### Understanding Property Cards

Each property listing shows:

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│     [Property Photo]        │
├─────────────────────────────┤
│ Property Title              │
│                             │
│ €250,000  |  2 Bed  |  1 Bath │
│ 75 sqm    |  Kypseli         │
│                             │
│ [View Details]  [❤️ Save]   │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

- **Photo:** Primary property image
- **Title:** Short description
- **Price:** Listed price in euros
- **Bedrooms/Bathrooms:** Number of rooms
- **Size:** Square meters (sqm)
- **Area:** Neighborhood location
- **Actions:** View details or save to favorites

## Expressing Interest

### Contacting About a Property

When you find a property you're interested in:

1. **Click "Contact" or "I'm Interested"**
   - Button located on property details page

2. **Fill Out the Inquiry Form**

   Required fields:
   - **Your Name:** Full name
   - **Email Address:** For agent to respond
   - **Phone Number:** Preferred contact number
   - **Message:**
     - Ask questions
     - Request a viewing
     - Express specific interest

   Example message:
   ```
   Hello, I'm interested in viewing this property.
   I'm available for a showing next week.
   Please contact me to schedule.
   ```

3. **Submit Your Inquiry**
   - Click "Send" or "Submit Inquiry"
   - You'll see a confirmation message

4. **What Happens Next?**
   - Property owner/agent receives your inquiry
   - They'll contact you via email or phone
   - Response time: typically 24-48 hours

### Tips for Good Inquiries

✅ **Do:**
- Be specific about your interest
- Mention your availability for viewings
- Ask relevant questions (price negotiable? when available? etc.)
- Provide accurate contact information

❌ **Don't:**
- Send multiple inquiries for the same property
- Use offensive language
- Provide fake contact information

## Property Viewing Checklist

When you go to view a property in person, consider checking:

### Interior
- [ ] Overall condition and cleanliness
- [ ] Natural lighting
- [ ] Storage space (closets, cabinets)
- [ ] Appliances (if included)
- [ ] Plumbing (check faucets, toilets)
- [ ] Electrical outlets
- [ ] Heating/cooling systems
- [ ] Noise levels

### Exterior & Building
- [ ] Building condition
- [ ] Common areas (if apartment)
- [ ] Parking availability
- [ ] Outdoor space (balcony, garden)
- [ ] Security (locks, gates, lighting)
- [ ] Elevator functionality (if applicable)

### Neighborhood
- [ ] Proximity to public transport
- [ ] Nearby shops and services
- [ ] Schools (if applicable)
- [ ] Safety and cleanliness
- [ ] Noise levels at different times
- [ ] Parking in area

### Questions to Ask
- Why is the current owner selling/leaving?
- When is the property available?
- What's included in the sale/rent?
- What are the monthly expenses (utilities, HOA fees)?
- Any recent renovations or issues?
- Is the price negotiable?

## Glossary of Real Estate Terms

- **Sqm (Square Meters):** Unit of area measurement (1 sqm = 10.76 sq ft)
- **For Sale:** Property available for purchase
- **For Rent:** Property available for lease/rental
- **Listing:** A property advertisement
- **Inquiry:** Your request for information about a property
- **Studio:** One-room apartment (combined living/sleeping area)
- **1-Bedroom (1BR):** Apartment with one separate bedroom
- **Ensuite:** Bedroom with attached bathroom
- **HOA (Homeowners Association):** Community organization (for condos/apartments)
- **Common Areas:** Shared spaces in a building (lobby, hallways, etc.)
- **Utilities:** Water, electricity, gas, internet services
- **Deposit:** Upfront payment (usually 1-2 months rent)
- **Lease:** Rental contract
